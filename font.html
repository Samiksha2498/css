FONT - FAMILY

examples of different font with their descriptions:

  • Arial, sans-serif:

    • A widely used sans-serif font known for its clean and modern appearance.

  • Times New Roman, Times, serif:

    • A classic serif font commonly used in printed materials such as newspapers and books.

  • Courier New, Courier, monospace:

    • A monospaced font often used for displaying code or other fixed-width text.

  • Verdana, Geneva, sans-serif:

    • A sans-serif font designed for improved readability on screens, commonly used in web design.

  • Georgia, serif :

    A serif font designed for on-screen readability, often used for body text on websites.
